Ice Cubes

Description

For people who don't know how to make ice when the ice tray is empty

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • tablespoons water (additional if needed)

Steps

  1. Take the trays over to the sink and fill them with cold water. (Hot water will freeze faster and more clear).
  2. Place the water filled ice trays back in the freezer.
  3. Shut the door to the freezer.
  4. Be sure to leave for around 4-6 hours at least to make sure it is frozen.
